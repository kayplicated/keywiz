The Receipt
The bill came to $47.82, which included a 15% tip and 2 coffees at $4.50 each. She pulled out her phone and checked the app: transaction #8821, posted 3 minutes ago. The café had been open since 1987, but only started taking cards in 2019. A small sign near the register read "$5 minimum, no exceptions." She tapped her card twice and waited for the beep.
